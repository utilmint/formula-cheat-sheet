Algebra

Quadratic Formula

Finds the roots of a standard quadratic equation ax² + bx + c = 0.

x = (-b ± √(b² - 4ac)) / (2a)
x: Unknown Roots
a, b, c: Coefficients (a ≠ 0)

Logarithm Change of Base

Converts a logarithm from base b to a new arbitrary base k.

log_b(x) = log_k(x) / log_k(b)
b: Original Base
k: Target Base
x: Value

Calculus

Power Rule of Differentiation

Derivative of a variable raised to a constant real power n.

(d/dx)[xⁿ] = n · xⁿ⁻¹
x: Independent Variable
n: Constant Exponent
Solve in Calculator

Fundamental Theorem of Calculus

Relates definite integration with antiderivatives.

∫[a to b] f(x) dx = F(b) - F(a)
F(x): Antiderivative of f(x)
a, b: Limits of Integration

Trigonometry

Pythagorean Identity

Fundamental relationship between sine and cosine for any angle.

sin²(θ) + cos²(θ) = 1
θ: Angle (Radians or Degrees)

Sphere Volume

V = (4/3) · π · r³
V: Enclosed Volume
